Welcome to FuturProctor!

FuturProctor is an AI-powered in-browser proctoring platform designed to ensure fair and secure online exams. It uses advanced machine learning models to detect cheating activities such as the presence of mobile phones, books, multiple faces, and unauthorized sounds. With real-time monitoring and automated reporting, it empowers educators with actionable insights effortlessly. With seamless functionality and robust privacy, FuturProctor redefines exam integrity with cutting-edge technology.

How to Use FuturProctor

Follow these steps for a smooth experience:

  • Login: Sign in using your registered credentials..
  • System Check: Ensure your webcam, microphone, and internet connection are working properly.
  • Start Exam: Begin the proctoring process by clicking "Start Exam."
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Allow monitoring through your webcam and microphone.
  • Submit Exam: Submit your answers once finished, and the system will generate a report.

System Requirements

Ensure your system meets the following requirements:

  • Device: Laptop or Desktop (not supported on mobile devices).
  • Browser: Latest version of Chrome or Firefox.
  • Operating System: Windows 10 or macOS 10.13 or later.
  • Internet Speed: Minimum 2 Mbps for smooth performance.
  • Webcam & Microphone: Integrated or external required.

FAQs

Common questions about FuturProctor:
